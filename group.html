The FIKE (Financial Information & Knowledge Engineering) group, pronounced /'faɪkə/, is not a Lab, but a decentralized interest group, where Frank Xing is just its mascot.
The shared vision of FIKE is (1) to discover new knowledges and (2) to tackle new IT challendges, for the finance industry.
