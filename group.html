The FIKE (Financial Information & Knowledge Engineering) group, pronounced /'faɪkə/, is not a Lab,
but an interest group conceived by Frank Xing.

The shared mission of FIKE is to:
(1) advance understanding of finance in the digital era
(2) solve intellectual challenges and shape the future for the finance industry.
